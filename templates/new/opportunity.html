Arnawaz Vasudev Charities

A private trust, was set up by S.G. Vasudev following the untimely death of his artist wife Arnawaz in 1988. The principal objectives of the Trust are to promote art education and art activities, and to provide financial assistance to deserving young artists and art students. The founding trustees are: Shanta Guhan, Girish Karnad and S.G. Vasudev.

For financial assistance/scholarships for art students :

Send a letter of request along with a proposal, an up-to-date biodata, 6 photographs of selected art works, and a statement regarding financial status/needs by post/courier to one of the addresses below:

Contact details: S.G. Vasudev Managing Trustee Arnawaz Vasudev Charities 2/143 Cholamandal Artists’ Village, Injambakkam Chennai 600 041 Or S.G. Vasudev Managing Trustee Arnawaz Vasudev Charities 71, S.T. Bed Layout Koramangala, Bangalore 560 034

E-mail: infoscholarships@vasudevart.com

Foundwork Artist Prize

The Foundwork Artist Prize is an annual juried award that we inaugurated in 2019 to recognize outstanding practices by contemporary artists. Starting in 2021, the Foundwork Artist Prize will honor two honorees, both of whom will receive unrestricted $10,000 grants and studio visits with each of this year's distinguished jurors. In order to be considered for the 2021 Foundwork Artist Prize, you will need to have an artist profile on Foundwork, with six (6) or more published artworks on your profile page, throughout the entire selection period: 5:00 pm PT, October 10, 2021 – 5:00 pm PT November 30, 2021.

GOVERNMENT OF INDIA Scholarships to Young Artists in Different Cultural Fields

This scheme focuses on assisting young artists of outstanding promise for advanced training within India.

Offered by: Ministry of Culture,

Deadline: Varies

Requirements :

  • Indian nationals in the age group of 18 – 25 who have already acquired a degree of proficiency in the chosen field.
  • The fields of Indian Classical Music, Indian Classical Dance, Theatre, Mime, Visual art, Folk, Traditional and Indigenous Arts, and Light Classical Music are covered here.
  • Must have undergone a minimum of 5 years training with their Guru/Institutes.
  • Candidates should have adequate knowledge in the connected arts/ disciplines.

What it covers : INR 5000 per month for two years
